Level

Level 1 starts with 1 feat, 4 spendable skill points, 0 combat skill points, and the Level 1 attribute cap. Fresh sheets begin all attributes at d4.

  • Level 1: 1 feat, 4 skill points, max upgraded attributes of 2d10, 1d8, and 2d6; all remaining attributes stay d4.
  • Levels 2+: 3 skill points and 1 combat skill point per level.
  • Feat progression: gain 1 feat every other level after Level 1.
